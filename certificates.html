Cisco Ethical Hacker

Cisco

Issued on: 30/12/2024

Cisco Ethical Hacker is designed to prepare me with an Ethical Hacker skillset and give me a solid understanding of offensive security. I will become proficient in the art of scoping, executing, and reporting on vulnerability assessments, while recommending mitigation strategies. Follow an engaging gamified narrative throughout the course and get lots of practice with hands-on labs inspired by real-world scenarios. By understanding the mindset of threat actors, I will be able to more effectively implement security controls and monitor, analyze, and respond to current security threats.
